Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vaulton really free to use?

Yes, Vaulton is completely free and open-source under the MIT license. You can use it for personal or commercial purposes without any cost.

How does the zero-knowledge encryption work?

Your data is encrypted on your device before being stored. Only you hold the encryption keys, meaning we have zero knowledge of your passwords or sensitive information.

Can I host Vaulton on my own server?

Absolutely! Vaulton is designed to be self-hosted. You can deploy it on your own infrastructure, whether it's a home server, cloud instance, or enterprise environment.
